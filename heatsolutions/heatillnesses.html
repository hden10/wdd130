HEAT ILLNESSES

Heat Cramps

There are different types of heat illnesses that show similar symptoms to each other. The least-severe illness is heat cramps. Heat cramps are when your muscles in your legs, arms, and core start to stiffen up. This comes from a loss of sodium from the sweat. Something that helps are pickles and pickle juice helps relieve the cramps.

Heat Exhaustion

Another illness is Heat exhaustion. This is from too much activity in the sun and there is an increase of blood pressure and increase in temperature. This makes you feel weak. If you get dizzy, nauseous, and feeling super sweaty then you are most likely experiencing heat exhaustion. This is the medium-severe one.
