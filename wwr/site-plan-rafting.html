Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to allow people to book appointments to be taken out in the the wonderful wilderness. It also gives tips on how to make your camping, rafting, and outdoor experiences more beneficial for the customer.

Audience

Our audience are people who love the outdoors. They enjoy being out in the vast country and enjoying God's beautiful creation. The colors we will use will match the outdoor schemes, like all the earth tones. Images of our adventures and all that we can offer, so that our audience will keep coming back.
